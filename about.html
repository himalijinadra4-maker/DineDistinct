Our Heritage

The Story of DineDistinct

Where every evening is a masterpiece in the making

Est. 2019 · Ahmedabad, Gujarat

Born from Passion,
Refined by Time

DineDistinct was conceived in 2019 with a singular vision: to create a dining experience that transcends the ordinary and becomes a cherished memory. Founded in the heart of Ahmedabad, Gujarat, the restaurant draws inspiration from the city's rich cultural tapestry while reaching outward toward global culinary traditions.

What began as a small 40-cover dining room has grown into Gujarat's most celebrated contemporary fusion destination — yet we have never lost the intimate, handcrafted spirit that defined our first evening of service.

Every element of DineDistinct — from the architecture of each dish to the curvature of each chair — has been chosen with deliberate care. We do not simply serve food. We compose experiences.

The Culinary Mind

Chef Arjun Mehta

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Trained at the Institute of Hotel Management Ahmedabad and refined through stages in Paris and Tokyo, Chef Arjun Mehta brings a singular perspective to the kitchen — one that sees no contradiction between a Gujarati dal dhokli and a French beurre blanc.

His philosophy is simple: the finest ingredient, treated with respect, tells its own story. The chef's role is merely to listen, and then to translate. Over fifteen years of cooking, Chef Arjun has developed a vocabulary that speaks across cultures and surprises even the most seasoned palate.

— Arjun Mehta
